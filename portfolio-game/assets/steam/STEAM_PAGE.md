## Full Description
**Zielony Pływak** (The Green Floater) is a cosy 2D side-scrolling cleanup game set on a sun-bleached Polish coastal island.

Play as **Kai**, an eco-volunteer who arrives by raft. The island is choked with plastic, chemical drums, and abandoned machinery. Your job: restore it.

### Features
- 🏖️ **5 interconnected zones** — Beach, Shallows, Meadow, Forest, Hill — each unlocking as the island heals
- 🦎 **12+ wildlife species** return as Biodiversity Index rises
- 📷 **Wildlife Photo Atlas** — photograph rare animals with the Drone Camera
- 🧰 **Tool Library** — borrow specialised equipment for different cleanup tasks
- 💬 **3 NPCs** with favour quests: Marta, Tomek, Baba Sela
- 🎵 **Layered ambient soundtrack** — music evolves with the island's health
- 🧘 **Mindfulness moments** — press [M] to pause and restore

### Tone
Calm. Hopeful. No fail state. Take your time.
